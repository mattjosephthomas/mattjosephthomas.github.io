What I Do

Two companies, one throughline.

Everything I build comes back to the same idea: make it easier for people to reach the places that matter. I take the boring infrastructure (both physical and digital) very seriously.

Outdoor Transportation & Experiences

Parkbus

Canada's national outdoor transportation company, operating from Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Halifax, and Ottawa. Express rides, guided ActiveDays, and overnight trips into nature. We recently signed MOUs with VIA Rail and Parks Canada, paving the way for a new era for the business.

Tourism Technology

Whereabouts

A destination management platform purpose-built for tourism boards. Helping destinations manage stakeholder relationships and market their destinations with clarity and ease. Winner of Tourism Innovator of the Year at the Tourism Industry Association of Ontario Summit.

About

Beyond the bio.

Being a father is easily the most fulfilling part of my life. I'm engaged to my fiancée Andrea, and the family we're building together is the thing I'm proudest of. I'm based in Ontario, Canada, and I've spent the past decade and a half working in and around the Canadian tourism industry. I've taught at Fanshawe College and Western University, and I hold a Master's in Strategic Foresight & Innovation from OCAD University and a certificate in Cultural Planning from UBC.

When I'm not working, I enjoy taking photos with my really old and trusty Canon 5D Classic (20 years old and still going strong), heading to the sauna, hot tub, spa, swimming, or doing other Pisces-type activities. I enjoy running, reluctantly lift weights, and try to leave the house as much as possible.
